The best movies according to Gabriel

My top 3 movies of all time

Love in the air

This movie is about a rich Igbo man and a hardworking girl who can provide for herself and dont want men who has more money. She is haunted by the experiences of her father towards her mother. And the Igbo man is willing to do everything to make her marry him and he is certain that she will be his wife

Gangster hood

A group of friends who grew up together planned to rob a bank, they were successful despite the chase of the police. Three years after they got caught by a new investigator who found a little trace of reckless spending

Selfish wife

A woman who was married to a lovely and fine man is selfish of her husband right, It got worst when she decided that she wont give their child breast to suck.
